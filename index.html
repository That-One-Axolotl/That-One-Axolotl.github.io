Hytale

I first saw Hytale all the way back when the trailer first came out, which was a REALLY long time ago. After that, I kinda went on with my life with Hytale in the back of my mind, while I just waited for it's release. Over the years, it went through multiple delays, was sold to Riot games, and was eventually cancelled. I first started actively following it again after the Riot acquisition. The cancellation hit like a truck, and came out of no where, but we still have hope, Simon could still save it

#SaveHytale

I am gratfull to have been a part of the #SaveHytale movement, we did so much, even now, before Simon has (probably) saved it, it is increadible what we did, our biggest acheivement was Wplace, where we made 2 massive artworks, a giant Kweebec (one of the species in the game) in LA, and a huge artwork of Simon outside of Quebec, with all our signatures around it

Eventually, our efforts were enough to get attention of Simon Hypixel himself (with some bullying from Tubbo), at the time of writing this, he is currently in active discussions with Riot Games about purchasing Hytale.
