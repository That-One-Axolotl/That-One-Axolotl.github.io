How it works

Basically, it just guesses a random word out of all of the possible words with the give information. It figures this out by storing the answers you gave it. when you input r for grey, it finds the letter it's corrosponding to and adds it to the array of disallowed characters. When its green or yellow, it instead stores them in a dictionary, with the letter as the term and the index as the definition. I do admit, this could have been done better because you can only store one index per letter, but I made this in like 30 minutes and I couldn't be bothered lol.

When it comes time to make a decision, it filters the list of potential words and removes any words that contain grey letters, as well as any words that don't have yellow letter (that are in a different spot than the stored index) and any words that dont have the green letters in the correct spot.
